# Business Manual

## Purpose
This showcase simulates how a 60-employee insurance company can transform core workflows with data, automation, and AI agents.

## Expected value
- Faster claims handling
- Better lead prioritization
- Reduced churn risk
- Clearer handoff between business and technology teams

## Showcase narrative
The project demonstrates how a realistic SMB insurer can move from fragmented manual operations to orchestrated, AI-supported workflows.
